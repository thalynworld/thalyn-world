The tool

Pick a feeling. Walk the world Thalyn builds around it.

You choose an emotional archetype and the shape of the world. Thalyn resolves everything else — terrain, biomes, water, erosion, paths, assets, atmosphere, and light — into a coherent, walkable 3D environment. The world is yours. You own the output.

No modelling. No scripting. No asset wrangling. Place what matters — a specific landmark, a focal point, your own imported model — and the world arranges itself around that intent. Then change its entire visual character with a single pick. Walk it again. Export it to Roblox.

Every copy of Thalyn includes a starting grant of Awen — the in-app credit used to generate worlds and collect visual looks from a growing catalogue. Your first session should feel like abundance, not a meter.

The feature no other tool has

Change your entire world's look in a single pick.

Walk your finished world in first person. Open the style selector. Pick a look — and watch the entire material character of every surface, every texture, every object in the scene transform at once. Not a colour filter. The whole world changes. This is live, real-time, and built into the engine. Every user has it from day one.

Awen — the in-app credit

Named for the Celtic concept of poetic inspiration.

Awen is Thalyn's in-app credit. Generating a world costs Awen. Every copy of Thalyn launches with a starting grant — enough for a generous first session without ever seeing a wall. The goal is that you're deep into your third world before you've thought about the credit at all.

Awen also collects visual looks from the open catalogue. The ten looks earned through ThalynQuest are a separate category entirely — Awen cannot buy them, and neither can money. They exist only at the end of the quest.

The starting grant is yours at account creation. No purchase required beyond the app itself.

ThalynQuest — optional, woven in, free

A treasure hunt hidden inside your worlds.

ThalynQuest is a free, optional discovery layer woven into Thalyn. Read the riddles. Shape the worlds they describe. Find the seven relics hidden in your creations. Claim them — and earn things money cannot buy.

Quest rewards include the power to create floating sky-island worlds, multi-emotion world composition, and custom audio environments — plus ten exclusive visual looks that exist nowhere else. These are free to earn and impossible to purchase. The most spectacular things Thalyn can create are walked for, not bought.

The tool is complete without ThalynQuest. A buyer who never touches the quest loses nothing from the core creation experience. The quest is woven in as a gift — not a gate.

Sky-island creation is a quest reward. Exploring a sky world someone else built is always free — any visitor can fly through one.
