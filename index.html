Questions

Things people ask.

Do I need to know Roblox Studio to use Thalyn?

You generate and configure your world entirely inside Thalyn — no Studio knowledge needed for that part. You'll want some familiarity with Studio to add your game logic on top of the exported world, but Thalyn handles the environment creation.

Can I use my own assets?

Yes. Thalyn lets you place your own assets as key locations the world builds around. The system assesses your assets for Roblox compatibility before export — if something won't work in Studio, you'll know before you get there.

Can I customise the world after exporting?

Absolutely. The exported file opens in Roblox Studio as standard content. Every element is editable. Thalyn gives you a strong intentional starting point — what you do with it from there is entirely yours.

What's Awen?

Awen is Thalyn's generation credit — a Celtic word for creative inspiration. Each world generation uses Awen. Your purchase includes a starter pack, and exporting to Roblox costs no additional Awen. Only the generation itself uses credits.

Can I sell games built with Thalyn worlds?

Yes. Your generated exports come with a full commercial licence. Use them in any Roblox experience, sell games built on them, modify them freely.

What's ThalynQuest?

ThalynQuest is Thalyn's built-in progression system. Hidden relics within your generated worlds unlock deeper creation tools and generation options. The more you explore, the more you can build.
