The export

Build the world once. Take it into Roblox finished.

In Thalyn you pick a feeling, and a whole world grows around it — terrain, trees, water, the light, the weather, all of it. You walk it in first person. You can change its entire look in a single pick. When it feels right, you carry it straight into Roblox.

Nothing flattens on the way over. The hills you climbed, the light at the hour you chose, the fog resting in the valley — all of it arrives in Roblox, walkable and playable. Sending your world to Roblox costs nothing extra. And it stays yours: yours to publish, yours to keep, yours alone.

Because your world began from your feeling and your choices, no one else lands on the same one. A player knows it's yours the moment they step in — not the terrain they've already seen a hundred times over.
