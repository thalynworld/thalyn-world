The export

Same world. Different platform.

Thalyn generates worlds that are inherently platform-agnostic. The generation brain produces a complete world manifest — terrain, biome data, asset placements, atmosphere, lighting — that a platform adapter translates into the target format. Exporting to Roblox costs no Awen. It is a pipeline operation, not a creative generation.

The result is a fully playable Roblox experience: walkable terrain, placed assets with correct physics, and complete lighting and atmosphere. Your world arrives as a scene ready to open in Roblox, with your visual character intact.

Thalyn is not a Roblox tool. It is the layer above platforms. Roblox is the first adapter. Others follow.

How it works
01

Generate your world in Thalyn

Choose your emotional archetype and world shape. Place any Hero Zones — your own landmarks or assets the world should resolve around. Thalyn builds the complete scene on its servers and delivers it to your client.

02

Walk it. Reskin it.

Explore your world in Thalyn's first-person viewer. Pick a visual look to transform the material character of the entire scene in one selection. Walk it again. Change it again. When it feels right, export.

03

Export — no Awen required

Thalyn's export pipeline translates your world manifest into a complete Roblox file. Terrain, asset placements, collision, lighting, atmosphere — all resolved. This step costs no Awen.

04

Publish on Roblox

Open your exported scene in Roblox Studio. Complete the Roblox platform steps — content rating, visibility settings — and publish as your own Roblox experience. Players can explore your world immediately.

What exports with your world

Terrain

Full heightmap, biome-resolved surface materials, natural slopes and water bodies.

Placed Assets

Trees, rocks, structures, and foliage — placed with emotional and spatial intent, correct scale and orientation.

Lighting

Time of day, ambient character, and atmospheric density resolved for your emotional archetype.

Atmosphere

Sky, fog, and weather behaviour carried through. The world feels the same on Roblox as it did in Thalyn.

Collision

Terrain and major assets include correct collision for player traversal.

Your Visual Look

The material character of the look you applied exports with the scene.
