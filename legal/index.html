Privacy Policy

Thalyn collects the minimum personal data necessary to operate the service. This includes account information (email address provided at registration), generation records (world seeds, configuration parameters, and saved world history associated with your account), and standard server access logs.

Thalyn does not sell personal data to third parties. Generation data may be used in aggregate, anonymised form to improve the Thalyn generation system. Thalyn Ltd is the data controller for personal data processed in connection with the Thalyn service. Data is processed on the legal basis of contract performance (operating the service you have purchased) and legitimate interests (improving the service).

Users have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of their personal data. To exercise these rights, contact hello@thalyn.world. Thalyn Ltd complies with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Thalyn's service is intended for users aged 13 and over. A date-of-birth check is presented at first run; users confirmed to be under 13 are blocked from creating an account.
