Section 04

Awen Credits & Service Terms

What Awen credits are

Awen is Thalyn®'s generation credit system. Each world generation consumes Awen credits. Your purchase of Thalyn® includes a starter pack of Awen credits. Additional credits can be purchased separately.

Exports and Awen

Exporting a generated world to supported platforms (including Roblox) does not consume additional Awen credits. Only the generation process itself consumes credits.

Service continuity

Thalyn® generation services depend on server infrastructure operated by Thalyn Ltd. While we are committed to maintaining these services for as long as the product is commercially viable, we cannot guarantee indefinite service continuity. In the event that generation services are discontinued, Thalyn Ltd will provide reasonable advance notice and any commercially reasonable alternatives available at that time.

Your exported content is not affected by service status. Worlds you have already exported and saved belong to you and do not require active Thalyn® services to use.

Unused Awen credits at the time of service discontinuation will be addressed on a case-by-case basis with pro-rata refund consideration where commercially reasonable.

In plain terms: what you have already exported is yours regardless of what happens to the service. Unused generation credits are the only thing at risk if the service ever closes, and we would handle that fairly.
