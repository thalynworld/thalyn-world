What you're seeing

Geometry only — the living layer is re-added here

A Thalyn export carries geometry and baked PBR materials. This viewer re-adds what doesn't travel in the file — sky, sunlight, ambient illumination, tone mapping, and the foliage wind shimmer. The result should look as close to the in-app experience as a browser can render.

The generation

Built from an emotion, not from a heightmap

No terrain was painted. No assets were manually placed. An emotional archetype was chosen — Serene, Eerie, Joyful — and Thalyn resolved terrain topology, biome distribution, asset placement, and atmosphere around that intent. This is what a Thalyn world looks like before a single human artistic decision is made.
